What is audiobooks:

Audiobooks are a set of features that allows users that might not have the ability to access certain books or accessibility capabilities. Audiobooks are for everyone, even if you have a busy schedule or especially when you need to multitask, that won't allow you to pick up a book. Audiobooks can help with reading comprehension and can also help you learn new words. br>


Audiobooks are made from people recording their voices and reading books that are popular and in high demand. Audiobooks started in the 1930s to give blind people the opportunity to listen to different books. Audiobooks were not for everyone, but by the late 1980s, audiobooks exploded and everyone was using them.


Audiobooks are considered a digital delivered contents that's that derives from spoken written language. Web standards are used to add consistency throughout the web using standardized web technologies like HTML, metadata, and resources that contained in a manifest.
